🚀 Latest Tesla News - 2025

Tesla's Global Sales and Market Dynamics

U.S. Market Share Decline: In Q1 2025, Tesla's U.S. vehicle sales dropped 8.6% year-over-year to 128,100 units, reducing its EV market share from 51% to 44%. Competitors like General Motors, Ford, and Volkswagen have gained ground, with GM's EV sales surging 94%, capturing 11% of the market. — Business Insider

European Sales Challenges: Tesla's sales in Europe plummeted 43% in early 2025, partly due to political backlash against CEO Elon Musk. European automakers like BMW and VW, along with Chinese manufacturers BYD, Xpeng, and Geely, are expanding their presence in the region. — Business Insider

🏭 Production and Tariff Impacts

Tariff-Induced Production Delays: Escalating U.S. tariffs on Chinese imports, now totaling 145%, have disrupted Tesla's production plans for the Cybercab and Semi models. The company has suspended shipments of Chinese components, affecting its goal to start trial production by October 2025 and mass production in 2026. — Teslarati, Reuters, DIE WELT

📊 Financial Performance and Investor Insights

Q1 2025 Earnings Preview: Tesla is set to release its Q1 2025 earnings on April 22. Investors are keenly interested in updates on Full Self-Driving (FSD) developments, the Cybercab project, and plans for more affordable EV models. — Teslarati

🌍 Global Expansion Initiatives

Saudi Arabia Launch: Tesla has inaugurated its first showroom and service center in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, marking a significant step in its global expansion. The launch attracted a large crowd, signaling strong local interest despite past tensions between Elon Musk and the Saudi government. — AP News
